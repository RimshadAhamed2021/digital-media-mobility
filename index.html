About us

We are here to create the new era of advertisement with you

Digital Media Mobility is set to establish the biggest smart digital outdoor advertisement mobile network in the KSA and MENA region. Allowing reach and engagement with the urban community through a remotely controlled real - time screen platform on the move 24/7.

Our network has multiple applications to radio, television, media, fashion, banking and other industries, granting content security audit and rapid response. Integrated media broadcasting, commercial advertising and geolocation technology in one place. Data statistics, monitoring and convenient cloud management for our clients to create, review and approve advertisement programs.

OUR PLATFORM

An easy and straightforward process to make a difference in your advertisement campaigns.

Digital Media Mobility platform supports program editing, auditing, release and other functions, the cloud platform can support millions of screen accesses with stable performance with no limitation by time and place.

PLATFORM FEATURES

Cloud controlled & secure system

The versatility of Digital Media Mobility grants several customization parameters while having a stable and synchronized network performance.

Content Security

Ensure security through user’s operation review. In case a broadcast accident occurs, our platform supports the controller’s logs review.

Real Time Platform

Large-scale urban reach through real-time screens network remotely controlled. You have the option to run multiple campaigns at the same time. You set the limitations for your business.

Integrated Control

Integrated media technology has applications in radio, television, media, fashion, road safety, and other industries. The utilization of the network goes from advertisement, emergency broadcasts, traffic guidance, and urban community indications with an incredible response time.

Easy to use

Cloud system provided for our clients to prepare their programs. Our clients can customize advertisement campaigns by time bracket and geolocation.
